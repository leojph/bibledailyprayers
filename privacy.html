Bible Daily Prayers is a Bible study companion: a daily word of Scripture, a place to write prayers, and a way to keep track of them over time. Because prayer is personal, we've built the app — and this policy — around treating what you share with care. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

CategoryExamplesWhy we collect it
Contact info Name, email address To create your account, sign you in, sync your prayers across devices, and respond to support requests
User content Prayers you write, prayer status updates (such as marking a prayer answered), notes, and reflections on the daily word To provide the app's core features: saving your prayers, tracking them over time, and personalizing your study experience
Usage data Feature usage, session length, device type, crash logs To fix bugs, improve performance, and understand which features are helpful

A note on the sensitivity of your prayers

Prayers and study notes can reveal your religious beliefs and other deeply personal matters. In many jurisdictions (including the EU and UK), this is treated as a special category of personal data requiring extra protection. Our commitments:

  • Your prayers are private by default. No other user can see them, and our team does not read them except when strictly necessary to resolve a support issue you've raised, or as required by law.
  • We process this content only with your consent, which you give by choosing to write and save prayers in the app. You can withdraw that consent at any time by deleting your prayers or your account.
  • We never sell your prayers or any of your personal information, and we never use your prayer content for advertising.

AI features

Some features may use artificial intelligence — for example, helping you shape a written prayer or offering reflections on a passage. When you use these features:

  • The text you submit is sent securely to our AI service provider to generate a response.
  • We instruct providers not to use your content to train their models where such controls are available.
  • Your name and email are not attached to content sent for AI processing beyond what is technically necessary.

How we use your information

  • To provide the daily word, prayer journal, and prayer tracking features
  • To create and manage your account and sync your content across your devices
  • To send reminders and notifications you've turned on (such as your daily word), which you can disable anytime in Settings
  • To respond to support requests and send important service notices
  • To monitor performance, diagnose crashes, and prevent abuse

Sharing with third parties

We share information only with service providers that help us operate the app — such as cloud hosting, analytics, and AI processing providers — and only to the extent needed for them to perform those services. These providers are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

Data retention

We keep your account and your saved prayers for as long as your account is active, so your prayer history is there when you return. If you delete a prayer, it is removed from our active systems. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where the law requires us to keep it longer.

Security

We protect your data with industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest for stored content, and strict access controls. No system is 100% secure, but protecting what you entrust to us is a responsibility we take seriously.

Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live (including the EU/EEA, UK, and California), you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, and withdraw consent

You can delete individual prayers or your entire account directly in the app, or contact us at the email below and we'll handle it for you within the timeframe required by law.

Children's privacy

Bible Daily Prayers is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you in the app or by email before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top shows the latest revision.
