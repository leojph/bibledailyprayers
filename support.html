Frequently asked questions

Can anyone else see my prayers?

No. Your prayers are private by default — no other user can see them, and our team does not read them except when strictly necessary to resolve a support issue you've raised. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How does prayer tracking work?

Every prayer you write is saved to your journal. You can revisit any prayer, add updates, and mark it answered when the time comes — building a record of your prayer life over time. Deleting a prayer removes it from your journal permanently.

I'm not receiving my daily word notification. What should I check?

First, make sure notifications are turned on inside the app (Settings → Notifications) and in your iPhone's Settings → Notifications → Bible Daily Prayers. Also check that Focus or Do Not Disturb isn't silencing the app at your chosen time. If it's still not arriving, email us and we'll look into it.

How do I delete my account and data?

Open the app and go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your personal data, including your saved prayers, is deleted or anonymized within 30 days. You can also email us and we'll process the deletion for you.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by the App Store. On your iPhone, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions, select Bible Daily Prayers, and choose Cancel. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Purchases are processed by Apple, so refunds go through them: visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the purchase. If you run into trouble, email us and we'll point you in the right direction.

How do I suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear it. Email us with "Feature request" in the subject line — every suggestion is read and considered for our roadmap.
